A short summary about me
and the people around me

My family

I grew up as an only child with just my mom in a small apartment in Amsterdam. Most of my family lives abroad and I unfortunatly hardly get to see them. On the picture from left to right you see my cousin, me, my aunt, my halfsister, my dad and last but certainly not least, my mom. I only got to meet my dad's part of the family for the first time not even a year ago. Which still to me is crazy to think about. I especially got along with Inbar, my cousin. She is one of the few people I genuinely treasure from my family. We bonded very quickly in the short time I got to see her. Since then, she has come to visit me twice in the last year and we still check up on eachother on a daily bases. I truly view her and love her as a sister and I'm proud to call her family.

My friends

There are very few things I care more about than my friends. They have stuck by my side ever since i can remember. We have all seen eachother at our lowest points and have carried and guided eachother through every situation. These are the people I'd bury a body for and I could not be more grateful I get to share my life with them.
